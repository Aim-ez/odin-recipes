Easy Peasy Spaghetti

Just get home from a twelve hour shift and the thought of cooking makes you want to drive your car off a bridge? Don't worry, there is a meal you can make that isn't just a mediocre frozen pizza that requires just as little effort.

Ingredients

  • 500g Package of Dry Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Medium Onion, Diced
  • 5 White Mushrooms, Sliced
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  • 28oz Prego Tomato Sauce
  • 2tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • 2tsp Garlic Powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, add ground beef and cook on medium-high heat until it is browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and 1tbsp of butter to the skillet, and cook until onions are translucent. Add the beef back into the skillet, add mushrooms, and stir.
  3. Transfer the skillet mixture into a large pot. Add the tomato sauce and spices to the pot.
  4. Bring the sauce to a simmer and then lower heat. Cook for 15 minutes, stirring often.
  5. While the sauce is cooking, add spaghetti noodles to salted, boiling water and cook them to al dente. stir often to prevent sticking.
  6. Drain noodles into a colander and rinse with cold water, separating any noodles that are stuck together.
