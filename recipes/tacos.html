Quick as Schmidt Tacos

Kids constantly bombarding you with supper requests but exhaustion abounds? Look no further, as the following taco recipe is ridiculously easy and can be put together within twenty minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Old el Paso Taco Seasoning Packet
  • 1lb Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
  • Tortillas
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream
  • Lettuce, Chopped

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, add ground beef and cook on medium-high heat until it is browned.
  2. Add seasoning packet, and stir, using the excess fat as liquid for stirring in the seasoning.
  3. Place cheese, tortillas, salsa, sour cream, and lettuce on the counter.
  4. Get the kids to form a line and fill their tacos with whatever toppings they like!
